Reptilian

Anthromorphic Crocodiles and Alligators who embrace raw power over intelligence and stand strong to their virtues.

Gender traits: Male: Teeth and Claws, Female: Gnaw and sharp scaled finger tips.

Crocayus wanted to spread the gift of knowledge he had received too other Reptilians. As those Reptilians grew up, they were smarter than the previous one, and for some reason, their bones were slowly restructuring, during this weird age, Reptilians would have many structural bone problems that led to not much Evolution being made. But then they finally got out of that phase, and the species became bipedal altogether. Being Bipedal is where their species growth would really begin. They proceeded to go through 300 years of evolution, becoming smarter and building their culture altogether. While the smart aspect of them has recently stopped growing, that raw power nature they have had since their creation will forever persist with them too the end of time.

Overall, the species thrives through building structures and ensuring almost every Reptilian has a place to work, thriving off of raw strength alone.

Roles

Learn how each character shapes the story through their unique purpose and power.

Hero

"A shining light that shows hope and determination."

Story Impact: The Main Hero's of the story, filled with determination and have the hearts of gold! They will do whatever it takes to achieve their goals and make the world ultimately a better place! The role is given too members of Crizion's squad!

Defender

"A resemblance of protection and sacrifice for anyone that does good."

Story Impact: Strong and unrelating fighters that will do whatever it takes to defend what they love for a good cause. The Role is commonly given too those that have protective or combative skills in their respective kingdom/home.

Nomad

"A firey passion to do better for everyone around them."

Story Impact: An advanced version of townsfolk that have special intellegence outside of your average townspeople, or have unique knowledge in certain fields of learning. These are commonly given to those who play important roles of information in their respective kingdoms/homes.

Netrual

"A showing of light and dark, mixed as one."

Story Impact: The balance between good and bad, often refered too as anti-heros. These kinds of people will commit good or bad actions depending on what their goals or benifits are. These are given too characters who are seemingly outcasted or mysterious.

Servent

"A loyal puppeteer, until death will they part."

Story Impact: Those who serve under whomever they are loyal too, no matter what. They will go out of their way to fullfill their masters commands, or whoever may guide them in the time. This is commonly given too people who have lost their way or are just...indecisive.

Opportunist

"A crude and fierce set of foes, with a sprinkle of mercy."

Story Impact: The bad of the story, the ones who stir up major trouble for their own or others benifit. But despite being bad, they still have hearts for something they love dear, showing a small sliver of good in them. This is commonly given too the bad guys/gals of the story who are misunderstood, or wish too see personal wishes come true through any means.

Villian

"Lost souls, damned with eternal pain to share with others."

Story Impact: The heartless most brutal of them all. These characters will go out of their way to get what they want and stop at nothing, no sacrifice is to big to fill the empty holes in their hearts, and they will likely never be redeemed. These are given to the main bad guys of the story.
